Conditions:

Stat conditions:
if you have 10/10 atk, you cannot have over 7 def
if you have 10/10 def, you cannot have over 5 atk
If you have 10/10 vitality, you will have max damage capped at 8/10 regardless of stats
If you have 10/10 vitality, you will have 50 more max hp. (Every tick is 5 hp)
If you have more than 5 vitality, you will have 5% more heal and revive. (Every other tick is 5%)
If you have 0 vitality, you will have a 10% chance not to heal at all.

Move conditions:
* You cannot have over 2 normal Magic Moves and 5 reserve Magic Moves
